Wallet ID simplified: Create a Smpl Handle!

Before you can enter your dashboard, you must create a handle. A handle is a simplified username that allows others to find and transact with you without the need to input your wallet address. Create something fun that represents you or your business!

  • Step 1 : In the handle input field, create a Smpl Handle
  • Step 2 : Once you have created your Smpl Handle, tap the SAVE button

How to send USDse

Now that you’re familiar with your dashboard, learn how to send USDSE to another user’s Smpl Handle by following these steps:

  • Step 1 : Tap the SEND button on your dashboard
  • Step 2 : Enter another user’s handle
  • Step 3 : Select user
  • Step 4 : Enter the USDse amount
  • Step 5 : Enter the memo
  • Step 6 : Tap the Send button
